ABOUT US

Welcome to DeviceWise, a cutting-edge online auction platform dedicated to bringing buyers and sellers of used electronic devices together. Our mission is to simplify the process of selling your old gadgets while ensuring buyers get the best deals on quality products. Whether you're looking to auction off a laptop, phone, TV, or gaming console, our platform provides an intuitive and efficient solution for all your needs.


How It Works?

Step 1: List Your Device

  • Start by selecting the company, model, year, and storage of your device.
  • Our intuitive interface ensures you provide all necessary details easily.

    • Step 2: Market Value Estimation

  • We generate the minimum current market value of your device.
  • Use this suggested price as the starting point for your auction to attract potential buyers.

    • Step 3: Create Your Auction

  • Give your auction a unique name or title.
  • Set the end date and time for your auction, then click POST to go live.

    • Step 4: Get Notified

  • Stay updated with every bid on your auction through email notifications.
  • Enable text message alerts for real-time updates if desired.

    • Step 5: Finalizing the Sale

  • When the auction closes, the highest bid wins.
  • If the buyer opts for doorstep delivery, they can enter their shipping details.
  • We provide shipping charges, with FREE shipping for bids over $1,000.
  • As the auction owner, choose between our company-provided shipping service or arrange your own.
